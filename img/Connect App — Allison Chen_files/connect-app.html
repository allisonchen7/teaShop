A term project for an Interaction Design Methods course. 

I led a team of 3 as we created, analyzed and interpreted research collected from members of the SMASH badminton club.

The Connect App is designed to foster the relationships between club members through IM, posts and events based around club activities. 

Role: UI Design, UX Design, UX Research, Team Lead

Inspiration

Inspired by how friendships are formed we created an app that fosters social interactions based around badminton. After all, our closest friends are those we spend quality time chatting with, not just playing a game with. It's in those moments between and after game play that we find companionship. And that is what Connect aims to achieve.

Challenges

We had some challenges creating a solution that would work for our group. Although we knew our design problem was to foster relationships between established and new members, we struggled with finding the right design solution. We played with a training app, or a club community board. But in the end we realized relationships are fostered in casual social interactions. This set us back quite a bit, we scrapped our previous UI and created this app within a few days.
